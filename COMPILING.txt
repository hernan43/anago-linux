Anago has been ported to the Linux platform and is known to compile under GCC
version 4.7.2 on the x86_64 platform.

--Dependancies--
libusb-0.1-4 (POSIX Systems)
libusb-win32 (Windows)
SQUIRREL3
kazzo

--Compilation--
 1. Copy the libusb, LibUSB-Win32, SQUIRREL3 and kazzo directories into the
    distribution directory as appropriote for your platform.
 2. cd into "src" directory
 3. Execute "make"
